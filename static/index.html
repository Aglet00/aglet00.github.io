About Me

Hey there!! My name is Christian Bush and I'm a developer😁

I was born in Woodland, California and later moved to rural Illinois when I was 9. What a transition! My high school was extremely small and the only vocational coursework offered there was related to agriculture. I knew absolutely nothing about computer science, had never programmed, and had never even heard the word Linux before I started at Mississippi State University

Googling, youtubing, reading, I was trying to sponge up as much information as I could to keep up because I was absolutely terrified of failing. However, the harder I worked, the more I learned and I loved it; the challenge of approaching a complex problem, breaking it down to fundamental knowledge, and logically piecing that information together to create the solution was extremely satisfying! Courses like physics, algorithms, circuits all compile down to simple truths. Wow!!

Now, I've had the opportunity to build hardware, software, and firmware and I LOVE it. I'm a full-blown, 100% hardened GEEK spending my free-time listening to 'Syntax' web development podcasts, watching presentations on bleeding-edge programming tools, and learning more about my favorite language: Python 🐍. Next year, I look forward to graduating and working full time while, hopefully, attaining a Master's degree.

Thanks for reading,
Christian.
